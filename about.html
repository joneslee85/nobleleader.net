Committed to your highest virtues
and aspirations

Essentially we believe that all people have the capability to become Noble Inspirational Leaders. We all need to be "awakened" from our false perceptions about ourselves, about others and about the World around us. We can become our own Leaders-our own CEO. When we learn how to become the Leader of ourself we can then turn our attention to becoming leaders of others. Our Philosophy is that in order for organisations to truly thrive and to inspire true commitment, passion and drive from all their employees they need to create, develop and maintain a corporate philosophy. This Philosophy should be such that it will create a culture of "belonging" for all people. All people include all stakeholders from the Chairman down and across and into all the activities of the organisation. This Philosophy should create a sense of "belonging" for all people which is integral to workplace health and happiness.

We are supported by a talented group of practitioners in Australia and Asia. Our people can provide a range of self awareness and self developmental services that can truly "awaken" the best within you.

    Founder

    Anthony Foster is the Founder of the Program. He has come from a diverse background of industries and has held many senior executive roles. He has worked for large multi-national organizations based in Australia, Japan and the United States of America. He has headed his own consultancy practices operating all over the World. For many years he was a trusted advisor within the corporate environment and in recent years graduated to become a Mentor and Coach to many CEOs and Senior Executives. In recent years he has been a prolific writer on Virtues in the Business World and has recently published his first full book on this subject.
