Workshops & Programs

Leadership Workshop

The Noble Leadership Program is delivered in a range of Workshops specifically designed for both Public and Corporate In-House requirements. Whether it be 2-day non-residential or a 3-day residential all participants have the opportunity to take their Leadership Skills and Understanding to a new level of Virtue based integrity and professionalism. All Workshops identify and address the issues and obstacles that prevent us from being true fulfilled Leaders.

Team Dynamic Workshop

Perhaps the team is experiencing a lack of cohesion or even some level of disengagement. We recognize that this is one of the most prevalent issues in the workforce today. Our Team Dynamic Workshops can re-ignite and re-engage people in a variety of innovative ways. In part this is achieved within the Self Leadership Program methodology but it also addresses those issues that can cause and overcome the loss of team spirit.

Personalised Leadership Coaching

The Noble Leadership Program is also delivered individually. It can be delivered to you by a trained Senior Executive Mentor each week for a period of 10 sessions. Each session lasts for 60-90 minutes and participants will receive similar benefits to those attending the Residential Workshop Programs.
